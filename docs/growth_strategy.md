## Part 3: Artificial Intelligence Optimization (AIO/GEO)

AI search engines (like Perplexity, ChatGPT) prioritize authoritative, structured answers. We optimize for this by:

### 1. "Answer Engine" Content (AEO)

Create content that directly answers common questions to be featured in AI snippets.

- **Action:** Create a dedicated **FAQ Section** on the Services or Contact page.
- **Target Questions:**
  - "What is the difference between Oil and Thai Massage?"
  - "Does Thai Massage help with back pain?" (Answer: "Yes, targeted stretching relieves tension...")
  - "Is Nuad Thai covered by insurance?" (Be honest: "Check supplementary insurance").

### 2. Semantic Authority (E-E-A-T)

Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness.

- **Credentialing:** Emphasize the **Wat Pho Certificate**.
- **Visual Proof:** Display the certificate photo on the About page.
- **Citations:** Ensure consistent NAP (Name, Address, Phone) on `local.ch` and `search.ch` to serve as verification sources for AI.
